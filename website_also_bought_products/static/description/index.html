Website Also Bought Products

Display the buyers also bought products on your website

Increase your sales by displaying the buyers Also Bought Products on your website while purchasing. Add the products automatically or manually to the Also Bought Product list.

Information

User Guide
https://webkul.com/blog/website_also_bought_products/

Salient Features

  • Displays the list of the products to the customers that are also bought with any product.
  • Add the products as Also Bought Products manually.
  • Add the products automatically as Also Bought Products while confirming an order.
  • No searching used while loading the products on website hence the process is fast.
  • Display the Also Bought Products in a slider.
  • Add the product directly to the cart from Also Bought Products.

Website View

Configuration

When automatic option is enabled the products will be added automatically to the Also Bought Product list on confirming a Sale Order. Also, the products can be removed manually from the Also Bought Product list.
